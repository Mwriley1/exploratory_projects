Start

Deal a New Hand
  Shuffle deck
  Deal initial player and dealer cards
  Player option to hit or stay
    If hit
      evalute 21 or bust
        If no bust
          loop to player option to hit or stay
    if stay
      return
  Dealer option to hit or stay
    if 15 or more
      stay
    if less than 15
      hit
      if no 21 or bust
        loop to dealer option to hit or stay
  Evalue player versus dealer
  print win or lose message
  loop to main menu
Quit

End
